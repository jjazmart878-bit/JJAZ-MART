## Midjourney Settings to Use:
- **Model**: V6.0 or V6.1
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:5 for products, 16:9 for hero
- **Style**: Realistic
- **Quality**: High (--q 2)
- **Style Parameters**: --s 250 (stylized)

---

## After Generation:
1. Download as PNG with transparency
2. Upload to Cloudinary
3. Use in hero section via `backgroundImage` property
